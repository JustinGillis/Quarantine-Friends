About us:

This website was designed as a group project from Students at Coding Dojo. Main purpose of this website is to help people get supplies that they need during the COVID-19 crisis.

So how does this site work:

This website works by users placing pins on map in their local area where they saw supplies and other users voting it up/down if supplies are there

How do I start:

  • Click the "Find the goods!" green button at bottom of screen
  • Allow browser to use your location (this will move map to your area)
  • Look for the toilet paper Icon, that is where your supplies are
  • Be sure to vote up/ vote down if supplies are there
  • Hey you found some supplies that are not on map share the info and click on map to place icon for others to see
